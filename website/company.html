Built by an operator, for operators.

Independent, neutral, and on your side. Kelvex answers to your uptime, your record, and your costs, not to an equipment catalog.

Kelvex came out of years spent in data-center and industrial IoT monitoring, reading mismatched equipment, normalizing it, and making it provable. The cold chain has the same problems and almost none of the same tools. It still runs on per-site boxes, paper binders, and spreadsheets.

An operator with a handful of sites usually has a different controller at each one, a separate login for each vendor, and no single place that holds the whole picture. The data lives on boxes that overwrite themselves in days. When a load is disputed or a system has been quietly losing refrigerant for weeks, the proof is gone and the cost has already landed. The equipment vendors are not going to fix this, because their job is to sell more of their own equipment, not to give you a neutral record you own.

That is the entire reason Kelvex exists. It sits above whatever you already run, keeps the record permanently, and stays read-only so it can never change how your systems behave. It is the layer that works for the operator instead of the manufacturer.

We are opening early access to a first group of sites, with transparent month-to-month pricing and hands-on setup. If you run cold storage and you are tired of learning about problems only after they have cost you, we should talk.

— The Kelvex team
