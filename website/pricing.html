Common questions

The things operators ask first.

Do I need to replace any equipment?

No. Kelvex reads from the controllers you already have. There is no rip and replace, no new wiring, and no firmware change to your control systems.

Which controllers do you support?

The major OEM systems including Danfoss, Copeland, and Carel, plus anything that speaks Modbus or BACnet, plus older standalone controllers. If you are not sure what you have, send us the make and model and we will tell you.

Is there a contract or a setup fee?

No long contract and no setup fee. It is month to month, onboarding is included, and you can leave whenever you like with your record exported and intact.

How long does it take to go live?

Most sites are sending live data within the same week. We do the integration and mapping work, so your team does not have to.

Will Kelvex change my setpoints?

No. Kelvex is read-only by default. It records and proves, and it never writes to your controls. When you want to make a change, you reach your controller's own screen from inside Kelvex in a logged session.
