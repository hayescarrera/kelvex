The platform

One view of every site, and the proof to back it up.

Kelvex is read-only, vendor-neutral, and built to sit above whatever controls you already run. Four things make it the layer your equipment was never going to be on its own.

Record

A history that outlives the box on the wall.

Site controllers overwrite their own data within days, so the moment that mattered is usually gone before anyone looks. Kelvex keeps every reading for years and makes it provable. When a customer disputes a load or an auditor wants records, you produce a single export that shows any site stayed in range across whatever window they ask for.

Refrigerant and compliance

The record builds itself as the work happens.

Leak rates calculate automatically, every refrigerant add ties back to its repair record, and an AIM-Act-ready log fills in as technicians work. You see a system losing charge as a trend and a dollar figure long before it becomes a surprise invoice, and when an inspector asks, the report is one click instead of a week of compiling spreadsheets.

Operations

Run the operation, not just watch it.

Monitoring tells you something is wrong. Kelvex is where your team works through it. Operators, technicians, and finance each open to the view that fits their job while sharing one record. Alerts are tuned to tell a real fault from a routine defrost, so the ones that reach your team are worth acting on. A technician logs a refrigerant add or a repair from a phone in the engine room in seconds, and the same screen shows what the plant actually costs to run once you add your power bills.
